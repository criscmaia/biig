BIiG’s partnership with Harokopio University of Athens includes a series of accredited courses that cover a range of very interesting topics, including:

  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Psychology of Entrepreneurship
  • Technopreneurship
  • Innovative Technologies in Computing
  • Managing a Computing Project
  • Management Information Systems

BIiG’s courses developed in the UK were evaluated by the university’s Lifelong Learning Centre and were recognised to be up to the required academic standard and quality to receive 4 ECVET credits upon completion of the course’s taught component and associated assessment. Learners will have the chance to attend synchronous sessions delivered online, covering the course topics and then access the learning materials online. All courses are designed for self-paced study, meaning that learners can manage to balance a busy lifestyle and work commitments with flexible study. Each course is assessed through a portfolio that enables leaners to apply their learning on actual issues that they may face at work, as part of their role or in familiar settings.

BIiG’s academic team has developed an innovative workshop structure aiming at the development of young learners’ personal effectiveness. The workshop that is delivered face-to-face immerses children in game scenarios, where they have to face different ‘challenges’ with the scope to develop certain competencies and assess areas that require further growth. Children have the opportunity to experience a wide variety of activities and tasks that are adapted to the requirements of each specific workshop and the participants’ abilities and needs. Children work in teams of different sizes, while their interactions are observed and assessed. At the end of each workshop, each child is awarded a certificate of completion, while parents receive an evaluation report, highlighting strengths and explaining areas for further improvement. For a more ‘holistic’ experience of the workshop parents may opt-in for (i) video recordings of team activities, (ii) video diary including the child’s reflections, (iii) more detailed feedback with performance observations, and (iv) follow-up consultations. All services are GDPR compliant and require explicit permission for the collection and processing of participants’ data. The workshop focuses on the following development areas and associated skillsets:

  • Leadership
  • Teamwork
  • Communication
  • Collaboration
  • Coordination

BIiG’s services are aligned to the growing need for learning experiences that are flexible, personalised and contextualised for learners’ work settings. These services are designed primarily for the UK and Greek educational sectors, with the ability to adapt for overseas markets. Our services include the following:

In collaboration with British institutions, BIiG offers the possibility of completing the first 2 years of undergraduate programmes in distance learning with the possibility of enrolling in UK universities for the completion of the 3rd year of study and obtaining a bachelor’s degree. The final year can be completed in the UK by studying on campus, or via distance learning, reducing studying and living costs. The subject areas offered are Computing, Business, Hospitality & Hotel Management, and Health & Social Care. The same procedure is followed for postgraduate studies leading to an MBA. In collaboration with the lifelong learning centres of several universities from Greece, BIiG offers short- and long-term accredited training courses, in a wide range of disciplines. You can find more information here.

BIiG offers integrated "academy" services involving the structure and operation of an integrated internal academy for larger organisations and the provision of similar services "externally" for smaller firms. These services include, the identification of the organisation’s training needs, the design and implementation of appropriate training programmes the evaluation of the provided training, the deployment of continuous training cycles to upgrade the organisation’s employee qualifications and addressing any educational needs that may arise. You can find more information here.

BIiG offers university-level programmes from the United Kingdom, delivered either in English or Greek. These are short workshops that are delivered over a few days, contextualised to meet learners’ needs, intended primarily for professionals of different sectors and local organisations (e.g. chambers, associations, local communities). Emphasis is given on providing learning opportunities to learners from regions where accessibility to such training workshops is rather limited. Indicative subject areas include business administration, business management, e-commerce, customer service etc. You can find more information here.

BIiG unbundled its academic programmes to offer a wide range of training topics covered in half-day or full-day seminars. You can find more information here.

In collaboration with its educational partners, BIiG offers the innovative “Personal Effectiveness Development” programme to children aged from 6 to 16 years old. This programme is already delivered in UK schools as a personal development workshop. Children are assessed with respect to their abilities in the following themes: (i) leadership, (ii) teamwork, (iii) communication, (iv) collaboration and (v) coordination. The evaluation of children’s performance involves feedback forms, optional video recordings, self-reflections with emphasis on individual critique. You can find more information here.

BIiG offers a pioneering English language assessment method, created specifically for children. The assessment takes place online and children can logon from their homes with the supervision of their parents. Age groups covered by this assessment include nursery, reception and years 1, 2 and 3. The English tests cover specific knowledge areas and take place throughout the year. These tests help children who are preparing for formal language examinations, but also enable parents to understand the children’s progress, compare their performance with peers and determine how to further support them.

Operations

I am responsible for a number of operations across my directorate and the entire department, including the following:

  • Supporting the Head of Department in staff appraisals
  • Preparing the directorate’s input for the departmental Annual Monitoring Report
  • Proposing Articulation Agreements with University partners
  • Coordinating assessment and feedback at programme level
  • Checking programme assessment schedules
  • Checking attendance monitoring with programme data administrators
  • Liaising with BCS for student memberships and awards
  • Delivering recruitment and marketing events such as
    • Open Days
    • Applicant Days
    • Experience Days
    • Taster Sessions
  • Notifying programme team about learning support cases
  • Handling programme admissions
  • Dealing with programme administration issues (e.g. student cases)
  • Coordinating exam and coursework preparation across programmes
  • Coordinating preparation for assessment boards across programmes
  • Coordinating preparation for programme progression boards across programmes
  • Dealing with student exchanges
  • Responding to External Examiner reports
  • Requesting assignments for teaching resources including
    • Student Learning Assistants
    • Graduate Academic Assistants
    • Hourly Paid Lecturers
  • Organising welcome week and induction activities
  • Preparing programme-level marketing information
  • Coordinating student surveys (e.g. NSS, PTES)
  • Charing the Programme Voice Group (PVG) meeting
  • Liaising with Student Voice Leaders (SVL)
  • Managing personal tutor allocations
  • Completing programme planning forms (e.g. COVID-19 alternative assessments)
  • Processing programme admission applications
  • Producing the directorate’s Annual Monitoring Report (AMR)
  • Performing Programme Progress Reviews (PPR) and Reality Checks
  • Coordinating programme reviews and validations
